Analysis of ODU fee Structure

INTRODUCTION

This project aims at visualizing the ODU's current fee structure and comparing it with other universities in Virginia. We have considered all the major public and private universities in Virginia and provided an option to the users to compare their tuition rates with ODU. The users can also click on the university marker to analyse the fee rates of various public and private universities respectively.

Each student has to pay mandatory fee along with the Tuition. We have used Juxtapose view to help the users visualize this distribution over the years. Mandatory fee includes lot of other divisions, these divisions are different for different years. Stacked bar chart is used to study these divisions and how they varied across different years.

There is a considerable difference in the amount of fee paid by Instate and Outstate, graduates and undergraduates. We have made an attempt to visualize this difference in the form of interactive line chart. Also provided a barchart that shows the current (2016) tuition rates.

MOTIVATION

  • The project idea is taken from Mace & Crown.
  • Fee is one of the important points of consideration before choosing a university.
  • We were curious to know the breakdown of ODU fee structure and also study its trend over the years.
