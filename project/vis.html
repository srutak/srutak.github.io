VISUALIZATIONS


Visualization 1:
This Visualization is used to help the users compare ODU's tuition fee with other universities tuition. Users can click on the university markers to study the comparison. Public and private universities are identified with different hues. University markers are provided with different hues to distinguish between public and private universities. We have integrated Google maps for easy identification of the universities.

Visualization2:
We provided an option to the users to study the fee structure of individual public and private universities. Horizontal grouped bar charts are made interactive by enabling the users to select/deselect items on legend to view visualizations of their choice. Users can view details on demand by hovering over the bars. .



Every student pays mandatory fee along with tuition fee. This visualization helps in studying their division across different years. Juxtapose views are used for easy comparison. Users can also view the details on demand by hovering.



Mandatory fee consists of the following divisions:

  • International Student HealthInsurance.
  • International Student fee.
  • Transportation Fee
  • Student Health Fee
  • General Services Fee
  • New Student Transition Fee
  • Technology Delivered Course

Stacked bar charts are used to study how these divisions vary in different years. Also switch view option is provided to help the users study the mandatory fee trend altogether.



Line charts are typically used to study the trend. We have implemented interactive line charts to study the tuition trend of graduates, undergraduates both instate and outstate over the years. Users can select/ deselect to view visualizations of their choice. A bar chart is provided at the bottom to know the current tuition.
